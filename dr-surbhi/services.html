Clinical Services

Dr. Surbhi Bansal Agrawal provides specialized care across two major domains: Advanced Fetal Medicine for expectant mothers and General Medicine for chronic disease management.

Fetal Medicine Department

Diagnostic Scans

  • NT Scan: Early screening for chromosomal abnormalities.
  • Anomaly Scan (Level II): Detailed organ survey of the fetus.
  • Growth Scans: Monitoring fetal weight and well-being.
  • Color Doppler: Assessing blood flow and placental function.
  • Fetal Echo: Specialized heart screening for the baby.

Procedures & Genetics

  • Amniocentesis: Fluid testing for genetic diagnosis.
  • Chorionic Villus Sampling (CVS): Early genetic testing.
  • Fetal Reduction: For high-order multiple pregnancies.
  • Intrauterine Transfusion: Treating fetal anemia.
  • Genetic Counselling: Expert guidance for high-risk families.

General Medicine

Diabetology

Comprehensive diabetes care focusing on 'Insulin-Free' management where possible. Includes diet counselling, CGMS monitoring, and complication prevention.

Thyroid & Hormones

Management of Hypothyroidism, Hyperthyroidism, PCOD/PCOS, and other endocrine disorders affecting metabolism and fertility.

Gastroenterology

Treatment for Acid Peptic Disease, IBS, Liver disorders, and other acute/chronic gastric ailments.

Hypertension

Blood pressure management protocols to prevent cardiac and renal complications.

Infectious Diseases

Expert handling of viral fevers, dengue, typhoid, pneumonia, and post-COVID complications.

Wellness

Preventive health checkups, obesity management, and lifestyle modification coaching.
